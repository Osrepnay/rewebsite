adrian's cubing stuff

I started learning to solve a Rubik's cube towards the end of September 2022. I managed to solve a cube without any assistance for the first time on October 4th, 2022. Ever since then, I have been continually practicing to improve my speed.

Some facts about me:

  • I solve using the CFOP method.
  • Compared to most speedcubers, I'm not very fast; my average solve time is just under 30 seconds.
  • My main cube is currently the MoYu RS3 M 2021.
